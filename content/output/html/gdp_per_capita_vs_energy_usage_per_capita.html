Notes

  • Primary energy: Primary energy supply is defined as energy production plus energy imports, minus energy exports, minus international bunkers, then plus or minus stock changes. This is the definition used by the OECD where the primary energy supply data was taken from IEA which uses the same definition.
  • Primary energy is considered demand by a country. There are discrepancies with as the value is based on a determined thermal efficiency of the underlying energy sources.
  • Conversion used is 1 Toe = 41.86GJ
